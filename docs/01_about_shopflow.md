# About ShopFlow

ShopFlow is a fictional direct-to-consumer e-commerce company used as the
sample dataset throughout this workshop. It sells consumer goods across 7
product categories to ~800 customers in 12 countries, processing roughly
4,000 orders and ~12,000 line items.

The company runs three sales channels (web, mobile, retail) and tracks
returns, customer support tickets, and web sessions. All data is synthetic
and deterministic (seeded with 42).

ShopFlow's analytics team treats DataPilot as their AI colleague: they
ask business questions in natural language and DataPilot answers using
SQL plus a set of data-quality tools.
